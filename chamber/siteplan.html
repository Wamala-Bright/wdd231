EcoResearch Hub

Website Site Plan

1. Site Name

EcoResearch Hub

This name was selected because the website serves as a central platform (“hub”) for environmental and sustainability research, educational resources, and data sharing.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of EcoResearch Hub is to provide reliable environmental research information, sustainability articles, and project summaries for students, researchers, and the general public. The site will also highlight ongoing environmental studies and promote awareness of climate and conservation issues.

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I find current research articles about climate change and sustainability?
  • How can students access environmental data or learn about ongoing research projects?

4. Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#1b5e20): Used for headers, navigation areas, and footer.
  • Light Green (#e8f5e9): Used for page backgrounds.
  • Accent Green (#33691e): Used for headings, borders, and emphasis.

5. Typography

  • Roboto: Used for headings and body text to ensure readability and a modern appearance.

The Roboto font is applied throughout this site plan document to demonstrate consistent typography usage.
